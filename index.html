About Me

After graduating college, I worked at a small company as an Accountant and working at a small company taught me how to work with my co-workers whenever we needed to collaborate on something. While I enjoyed the learning opportunities I was given as an Accountant, I found myself feeling unenthusiatic at the thought of continuing to work in the Accounting field. I would like to think that I was given a second chance because I was able to explore my options in a different field.

I strategized what I wanted to do next and the thought of being a Software Developer came to my mind. I had taken a class in high school where I learned basic web development, I learned about the database software Access in a course in college, I also helped to code some webpages for my dad's dry cleaning company website, and I took some online courses that introduced me to basic coding languages. Through everything I did that revolved around Software Development, I remember always being interested by it. So, I thought I could try to become a Software Developer and thus, began my journey back into Software Development. I refreshed my memory by taking some online courses. All the while I also discovered online bootcamps, like Thinkful, which made me believe it is possible to get a fresh start.

Now I am a student at Thinkful and starting my journey to becoming a Software Developer which I couldn't be more ecstatic about!
